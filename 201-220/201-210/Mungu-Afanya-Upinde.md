## 208. Mungu Afanya Upinde
[edit](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1SeDZ753PZ41djwwF0j_dD46_VSiiG1Ts/edit?mode=html)




    1
    Nani afanya upindi,
    Namjua, namjua
    Mungu afanya upindi
    Nampenda kwa hiyo.

    2
    Mungu atuma upindi
    Ni bora mweupe,
    Mungu atuma upindi,
    Kwamba yu karibu.
