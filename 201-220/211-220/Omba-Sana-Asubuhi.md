## 217. Omba Sana Asubuhi
[edit](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1Rl3LQPNT27DYc8cZd%2DHnOn13EcurxPI_/edit?mode=html)




    1
    Omba sana asubuhi,
    Omba sana mchana,
    Omba sana na jioni,
    Bwana hutusikia.

    2
    Mungu hujibu maombi,
    Asubuhi na mchana,
    Hata hutungojea tena,
    Wakati wa jioni.

    3
    Na tuimbe asubuhi.
    Tena saa za mchana.
    Hivi tutafurahi Naye.
    Pumziko la jioni.
